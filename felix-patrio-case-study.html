Felix Patio

A Modern Cafe Website with Classic Cartoon Charm

The Story

Meet Felix, my mate who had a brilliant idea to create a cozy cafe with Felix the Cat as the brand identity. When he approached me to design the website, I knew this was a unique opportunity to blend modern web design trends with the nostalgic charm of classic cartoon aesthetics.

The challenge was clear: create a website that would appeal to modern cafe-goers while staying true to the whimsical, old-time cartoon character that Felix the Cat represents. We needed to balance contemporary functionality with vintage character design elements.

The result? A website that captures the essence of both worlds - modern cafe culture meets classic cartoon nostalgia, creating an unforgettable digital experience that perfectly represents Felix Patio's unique brand identity.

The Design Process

When designing Felix Patio's website, I focused on three key elements that would bring the Felix the Cat brand to life in the digital space:

Modern Cafe Aesthetics

I studied current cafe website trends to ensure the site would feel contemporary and trustworthy to potential customers. This included clean layouts, intuitive navigation, and mobile-first responsive design that modern users expect.

Classic Cartoon Character

To honor the Felix the Cat brand, I incorporated vintage cartoon design elements including playful typography, bold color schemes, and character-inspired illustrations that evoke the golden age of animation.

Brand Identity Integration

Every design decision was made to reinforce the Felix the Cat brand identity - from the color palette inspired by classic cartoons to the playful animations that bring the character's personality to life on screen.
