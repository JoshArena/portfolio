PerfectCuts

AI-Powered Haircut Recommendations

My first design project that taught me the power of simplicity. An app that helps users find their perfect haircut using AI face analysis.

The Story

PerfectCuts was one of my first design projects, and looking back, it perfectly demonstrates why simple designs work so well. The app uses AI to analyze your face shape and recommend haircuts that would look best on you.

I coded this app twice - first in React to prototype the concept and user flow, then rebuilt it in Swift for the iOS App Store. The simple design actually became a strength - users could clearly identify the main actions and understand the user flow without any confusion.

This project taught me that sometimes the best design is the one that gets out of the user's way and lets them accomplish their goal quickly and efficiently.

Business Model

Monetization Strategy

PerfectCuts was designed with a freemium model in mind. Users could download the app for free, but would need to purchase credits to get AI-powered haircut analysis.

Primary Revenue: In-app purchases for scan credits ($2.99 for 5 scans) - this was the main way users would access the core AI functionality.

Secondary Revenue: Affiliate marketing from product recommendations. When users viewed recommended haircare products and styling tools, I would earn commission from any purchases made through the app.

This dual-revenue approach ensured the app could generate income both from the core AI service and from helping users discover and purchase related products.
